CODE OF CONDUCT
This purpose includes its clients and customers and is achieved through the demonstration of the Xecurify and affiliates' values by those who interact with them.

A. Unity:
We acknowledge and uphold the inherent dignity of all individuals with whom we engage, treating them with respect and valuing their contributions.
We are collectively aligned in pursuit of a common objective, fostering cooperation and shared purpose.
We are committed to promoting diversity and ensuring inclusion, striving to create an environment where all individuals are valued and afforded equal opportunities.

B. Agility:
We undertake actions with promptness and make decisions in a timely and decisive manner.
We demonstrate flexibility, responsiveness, and a proactive approach to change and evolving circumstances.
We exercise prudent judgment in taking calculated risks when necessary, in alignment with organizational objectives.

C. Energy:
We are committed to driving positive change and continuously challenging established norms and practices.
We prioritize and support the development of individual leadership capabilities.
We foster a motivating environment by encouraging mutual ambition and inspiring one another to achieve collective goals.

D. Accountability:
We are committed to providing the highest possible value to our customers and stakeholders, ensuring their interests are met and expectations are exceeded.
We accept full responsibility for our actions and outcomes, maintaining accountability for the results we deliver.
We recognize and reward performance, acknowledging achievements in accordance with established criteria and organizational standards.

Accordingly, Xecurify encourages its customers to partner with it in achieving this goal, by ensuring that all their employees and subcontractors interact with Xecurify's personnel, clients, and customers on Xecurify's behalf, and demonstrate these values and behaviors.

This Code of Conduct is designed to ensure that customers align with Xecurify's philosophy in these areas and to encourage their adherence to the principles and values upheld by Xecurify.

E. Xecurify upholds the following principles and expects its customers to comply with them:
To comply with all applicable laws and regulations of the countries in which it operates
To support and respect the protection of internationally proclaimed human rights.
Not to be complicit in human rights abuses.
To uphold the freedom of association and the effective recognition of the right to collective bargaining.
Not to make use of any forms of forced and compulsory labor or child labor.
To prevent discrimination in respect of employment and occupation.
To support a precautionary approach to environmental challenges.
To undertake initiatives to promote greater environmental responsibility.
To encourage the development and diffusion of environmentally friendly technologies.
To work against corruption in all its forms, including extortion and bribery.

F. Xecurify intends to make these principles key drivers of its purchasing policy:

1. Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
Xecurify adheres to the principle that its business should be conducted in accordance with all applicable laws of the countries where it conducts its businesses and any other applicable foreign jurisdictions.

2. Environment, Social, and Governance (ESG)
Xecurify' ESG mission is to:
Deliver a high integrity ecosystem, for all our global stakeholders and our community, thereby enhancing the security and transparency of Xecurify's markets.
Respect and develop our human capital, which is critical to our business success, and equip our employees with appropriate Human Resources policies.
Drive and facilitate sustainable finance, foster dialogue between the different players in the industry and contribute to the financing of the real economy in its transition to a sustainable society.

3. Human Rights
Xecurify is strongly committed to the protection of human rights globally and is guided by fundamental principles, including those enshrined in the United Nations Universal Declaration of Human Rights, the International Labour Organisation (ILO) Core Conventions, and applicable provisions under Indian law.

4. Modern Slavery Act Statement
Xecurify Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates are committed to ensuring that there is no modern slavery or human trafficking in our supply chains or in any part of our business.

5. Diversity and Inclusion
Xecurify has a firm commitment to enabling equality of opportunity and workplace cultures that promote inclusion.

6. Business Integrity
Xecurify is fully committed to conducting its operations in strict compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and standards, including Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Compliance, Sanctions Compliance, Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption, and Intellectual Property Protection.

7. Reputation
Xecurify shall not engage in any conduct that may tarnish or harm the reputation or image of any company within the Xecurify group.

8. Customer's Acknowledgment
By entering into a customer relationship with Xecurify and purchasing products and/or services from Xecurify, the customer acknowledges and agrees to adhere to the principles and philosophy set forth in Xecurify's Code of Conduct throughout the duration of the relationship.
